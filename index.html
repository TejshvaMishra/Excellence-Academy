About Us

Our Mission

Founded in 1985, Excellence Academy has been committed to providing exceptional education for over 35 years. Our mission is to inspire and empower students to achieve academic excellence, develop personal integrity, and become responsible global citizens.

We believe in a holistic approach to education that nurtures the intellectual, physical, emotional, and social development of each student. Our dedicated faculty and staff create a supportive and challenging environment where students can discover their passions and reach their full potential.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Respect, Innovation, and Community are the core values that guide everything we do at Excellence Academy.

Our Programs

Academic Excellence

Our rigorous academic curriculum is designed to challenge students and prepare them for success in college and beyond.

Arts & Creativity

We offer comprehensive programs in visual arts, music, theater, and dance to nurture creativity and self-expression.

STEM Innovation

Our STEM program integrates science, technology, engineering, and mathematics through hands-on, project-based learning.

Athletics & Wellness

We promote physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship through a variety of competitive and recreational sports.

Years of Excellence

Excellence Academy has been recognized as a National Blue Ribbon School of Excellence three times. Our students consistently score in the top percentiles on standardized tests and go on to attend prestigious universities around the world.

Recent graduate Sarah Johnson was awarded the Presidential Scholarship for her groundbreaking research in environmental science, and our robotics team placed first in the State Championship for the third consecutive year.

Our Faculty

Our exceptional faculty members are the heart of Excellence Academy. With advanced degrees from prestigious universities and years of experience in their fields, they bring expertise, passion, and dedication to the classroom every day.

Over 80% of our teachers hold master's degrees or higher, and many are recognized leaders in their academic disciplines. They are committed to ongoing professional development to ensure they are implementing the most effective teaching strategies.

Beyond their academic credentials, our faculty members serve as mentors and role models who inspire students to pursue their passions and achieve their dreams.
